    Even though I was born in Canada, my mother tongue is Punjabi. Punjabi language originated from the north-west part of India and now it is the 4th most spoken language in Canada. Punjabi is important to me because it connects me with my ancestors and helps me communicate with my grandparents and people of the same language. At home I speak punjabi and english, mainly speaking punjabi with my grandparents and english with my parents, brothers and sister. Speaking this language also helps me connect to my culture.
