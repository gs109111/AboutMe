    Sikhism is a significant component of my identity because it influences my opinions, convictions, and sense of self. I truly enjoy observing Punjabi religious practices, especially the Nagar Kirtan (Sikh Parade) every year in Calgary. I am admired by the three core values of sikhism, which are meditation upon devotion to the creator, truth-full living and service to humanity. I respect all religions and support them through donations and services.I am not a ritualistic person but I am religious.
